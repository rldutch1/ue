UE Command Palette: Control + Shift + P
	01. Can just type what you are looking for.
	02. Type : to access Goto options.
	03. Use backslash to search through previously open files.
	04. Control + Up or Down arrow to move the entire page up or down.
	05. Wordwrap - Control + w
	06. Control . highlights all words under the file.
	07. Control ; select all words under the caret.
	08. Control , select all words under the caret so you can edit them.
	09. Alt + -
	10. Alt + F3 Find dialogue
	11. Alt + C enter column mode.
